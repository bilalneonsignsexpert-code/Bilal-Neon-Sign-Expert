About the Studio

Every sign that leaves this studio is bent, wired, and tested by hand — not mass-produced on a line.

Bilal Neon Sign Expert began as a one-man workshop in Lahore and grew through word of mouth into a studio trusted for two very different crafts: flexible silicone neon for homes, weddings and brands, and fine laser-cut Islamic wall art for the home. Both are built on the same standard — clean linework, durable materials, and a finish that looks as considered up close as it does from across the room.

  • Hand-bent silicone neon tube, not rigid glass
  • Low-voltage, cool-to-touch LED drivers
  • In-house design proofing before production
  • Custom sizing for any wall or storefront
  • Gold, black, and backlit finishes on wall art
  • Packed and insured for worldwide shipping
